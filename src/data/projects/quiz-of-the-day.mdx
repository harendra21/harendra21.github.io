# QOTD (Quiz Of The Day)

![Quiz Of The Day Screenshot](https://res.cloudinary.com/harendra21/image/upload/w_800/Screenshot_2024-09-18_at_4.07.52_PM_gpejk9.png)

## Overview
Aakash Digital’s Quiz of the Day is a daily feature on the Aakash App designed to help students preparing for NEET, JEE, and other competitive exams. It offers a new set of questions each day, allowing students to test their knowledge and track their progress. Participants can see how they rank on the leaderboard, adding a competitive edge to their preparation. 



## ✏️ Features of Aakash Digital - Quiz of the Day

- **Daily New Quiz** -
Get a fresh set of questions every day to practice regularly.
- **Exam-Specific Questions** -
Quizzes are designed according to the latest NEET, JEE, and Foundation exam patterns.
- **Leaderboard Ranking** -
See your All-India ranking after completing the quiz.
- **Instant Solutions** -
Get correct answers and detailed explanations immediately after submitting.
- **Performance Tracking** -
Track your daily scores and see your improvement graph over time.
- **Competitive Environment** -
Leaderboards create a healthy competitive vibe and boost motivation.
- **Mobile Friendly** -
Easily attempt quizzes anytime, anywhere through the Aakash app.
- **Free Access** -
Quiz of the Day is mostly free — just need to install the app.
- **Short Duration Quizzes** -
Quick quizzes with 5–10 questions, perfect for fast practice without taking too much time.
